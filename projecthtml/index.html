Kauvery Hospital

Kauvery Group of Hospitals launched its first hospital more than two decades ago. The founders of Kauvery Hospital were determined on creating world-class healthcare facilities that shall be affordable. The founding doctors set off on this dream in 1999 with a 30-bedded hospital in Trichy, with a single-minded focus on offering the best-in-class healthcare, with a personal touch. This was a very new concept in a tier 2 city like Trichy which lacked a tertiary care hospital at the time. Today, Kauvery is a multi-specialty hospital chain with 2250+ beds in six locations including Trichy, Chennai, Salem, Hosur, Tirunelveli and Bengaluru. Kathir Hospital

Founded in 2017 by Dr. Bharath Kumar (Orthopedic Surgeon) and Dr. Kathiroli (Gynecologist), Kathir Hospital has been a beacon of specialized medical care in Trichy. What began as Kathir Nursing Home in Thillai Nagar soon expanded into a state-of-the-art 24-bed multispecialty hospital on Salai Road, Trichy, in 2019, with the goal of delivering advanced, minimally invasive treatments. With a team of 15+ consultant doctors, Kathir Hospital handles over 10,000 patients annually through both in-patient and out-patient services. The hospital's core philosophy lies in leveraging the latest advancements in medical technology to offer precise, effective, and minimally invasive treatments, reducing recovery time and improving patient well-being.
